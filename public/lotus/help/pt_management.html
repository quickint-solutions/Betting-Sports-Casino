Position Taking Management

This section describes how you manage and view Position Taking for one or multiple MAs.

Using this feature, you can select one or more MAs and change Position Taking settings. In the Take column, you can set your own Position Taking whilst in the Give column you can set the maximum Position Taking that can be distributed by
the MA(s) that you have selected. You can also view how much position your MAs or Agents take against their downline users, but you cannot edit these settings

To modify Position Taking, follow this procedure:

To display the list of your MAs, click the Agency Management > Position Taking Listing menu item. The PT you have been given per sport is displayed above the table on the right.

To view how much position your MAs or Agents have taken against their downline users, click the button next to the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} to drill down as required.
To select all the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}}s displayed in the Position Taking Listing table, click the checkbox in the top left corner.

Alternatively, you can select users individually by clicking the checkboxes next to the their login names.

To change Position Taking for the selected users, click the Edit PT button.

Fill in the Take and Give fields making sure that the sum of the values in these two fields is less than or equal to your maximum Position Taking amount per sport. To confirm, click the Save button.

A notification on the top of the screen will let you know if the changes were applied successfully and the updated values are shown in green.

If the new Position Taking settings cannot be applied, a red triangle is displayed next to the login name of the user. Move your mouse over the triangle to find out why the changes are not accepted.
Note: You have to make sure that all the values that you enter in the Takeand Give fields comply with your maximum Position Taking settings, otherwise you will not be able to save your changes.
