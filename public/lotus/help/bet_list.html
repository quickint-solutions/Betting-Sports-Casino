Bet List

This section describes how to list all the bets the Members have placed in a given period of time.

Specify a date range using the date picker drop-down and click the Apply button to display the Bet List.

By default, the current day is selected in the date picker. You can also specify a time range or select one of the pre-defined date ranges.
The bet list shows the matched bets that are currently active in the system. Bets that are invalidated are highlighted in red.

Clicking the Unmatched radio button displays the list of bets that are not completely matched

To display the list of past bets, click the Past tab on the bet list screen. Invalidated bets are highlighted in red.

You can download all the bets in the selected date range by clicking the Download CSV button.
