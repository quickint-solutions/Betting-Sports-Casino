Creating a New {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Account

This section describes how to create an account for an {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}}.

Creating a new {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} is a quick and easy process in the site. You can edit additional user options, once you have created the new agency user.

To create a new MA, follow this procedure:

Navigate to the Agency Management > {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Listing menu item and click the New MA button.

The Username field in the Information section is already filled in automatically, but you can change this if you wish. Enter a login name and a password in the Information section. Also, set the user status to Active or Inactive.

Note: Inactive MAs cannot log in to the site.
Tip:
Fill in all available username fields using letters or numbers only.
A login name must be at least four characters consisting of lower case letters, numbers and dots ( . ). Other special characters are not allowed.
Passwords must be between 6 and 15 characters and must contain at least one number and one letter. Special characters are not allowed.
In the Credit section, enter the credit limit that the new {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} can distribute among their downline users in the Credit Limit field.

Note: When setting up the credit limit for an MA, the credit that you set in the the Credit Limit field will be transferred from your balance to theirs. Therefore, you must make sure that you have enough credit on your balance to transfer from.
Set up Position Taking by filling in the CUS Position (%) field that applies to you as well as the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Max Position (%) field that applies to the new {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} you are creating. For more information on Position Taking, see Position Taking.

You can add a note about the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} you are creating in the Notes section.

Click the Create button to create the new MA.
Note: You can edit additional user options by navigating to the Agency Management > {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Listing menu item and clicking the Login Name or the ID of the MA.
