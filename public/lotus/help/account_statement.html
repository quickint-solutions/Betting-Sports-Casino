Account Statement

This section describes how to view your account statement.

The account statement lists all the changes that have affected your balance. P&L Statement shows your profit and loss based on market settlement, whilst Credit Statement shows the changes in your credit limit as well as your available credit.

To view the account statement, follow this procedure.
Navigate to the Account > Statement menu item. By default, today's taken place today are displayed.
Specify a time frame using the
From and To drop-down menus and click the Search button to display the P&L statement for that time frame. The Description field explains why your account balance has changed.
Betting P&L - Settlement by market or by selection.
Betting P&L Comm Adjustment - Commission adjustment. Only happens for outright markets once the market is closed.
Betting P&L Adjustment - Market resettlement or invalidation of settled bets.
Click a market or selection to see the breakdown of the bets.
