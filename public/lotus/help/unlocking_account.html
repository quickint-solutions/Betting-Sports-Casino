Unlocking a Customer, MA, Agent or Member an MA Account

This section describes how to unlock the account of a downline user in the site.

Downline users may get locked out of the system if they have too many unsuccessful login attempts. To unlock the account of a Customer, MA, Agent or Member, an MA, you must reset their password.
Note: You cannot unlock an account that was previously closed. Closing an account is a permanent action that cannot be undone.

To unlock an Customer, MA, Agent or Member MA account, follow this procedure:

Navigate to the Agency Management > MA Listing menu item and click the Login Name or the ID of the MA whose password you want to reset.

In the Information section, enter and confirm a new password in the Password and Repeat Password fields.

Important: Passwords must be between 6 and 15 characters and must contain at least one number and one letter. Special characters are not allowed. Click the Save button to reset the password of the MA account. Parent topic: {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Management
