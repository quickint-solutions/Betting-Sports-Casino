Undo Transfer

This section describes how to undo a transfer between you and an MA.

In the Undo Transfer window, you can see a list of transfers made between you and your MAs. If you wish you can undo the most recent transfer with any of them.
Note: You can undo the most recent transfer per {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} only. Also, you cannot partially undo a transfer.

To undo a transfer, follow this procedure:

Navigate to the Agency Management > Transfer menu.

Click the Undo Transfer button in the top right corner of the Transfer pane.

Click the Undo Transfer link next to the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} whose most recent transfer you want to undo.

Click Yes to confirm to undo the transfer.
