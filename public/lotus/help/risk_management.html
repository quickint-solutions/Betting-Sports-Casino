Risk Management

This section describes how you can find out about your net exposure in different markets and market types.

There are two main features available under the Risk Management menu: Net Exposure and Outrights . Values in the Stake column show how much has been staked against you based on your Position Taking. Red or green values in the 1 ,
X , 2 or P&L column show how much you can lose or win, respectively.

Select the Net Exposure option to find out about the potential worst case scenario of how much you can lose or win based on the currently active event markets.

Select the Bet Ticker option to list all the bets the Members have placed in a chronological order.

To find out more about the Bet Ticker, read the Bet Ticker section.

Select the Outrights option to find out about your net exposure in outrights markets.
