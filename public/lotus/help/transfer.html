Transfer

This section describes how to carry out a transfer between you and an MA.

In the Transfer window, you can get an overview of the credit status of your MAs.

The green number in the Take column shows how much the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} owes you, while the red in the Give column shows how much you owe the specific MA.

If the transfer between you and the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} has taken place, you can make a note of this by following this procedure:

Navigate to the Agency Management > Transfer menu.

Click the Transfer link next to the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} you have performed a transfer with.

Enter the transferred amount in the Transfer amount field and click the Transfer button. By default, the total balance is shown in the Transfer amount field but you can freely change it.

You can see an updated value for the specific user in the Bal.(Up) column.
