Net Exposure

This section describes how you can find out about your and your downline's net exposure.

By default, this table shows your Net Exposure according to your position taking on all the markets where your downline Members have placed bets.

To show the summary of all the bets on a market, click the Total Book radio button.

To show all the bets separately that were placed on a given market, click the button.
