Closing an {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Account

This section describes how to close an {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} account.

Important: Closed status cannot be changed. Users with a closed account are locked out of the system permanently which means that they or their downline will not be able log in to the system.
To close an {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} account, follow this procedure:
Navigate to the Agency Management > {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Listing menu item and click the Login Name or the ID of the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} account which you want to close.

In the Information section, change the Status of the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} to Closed.

Click the Save button to close the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} account.
