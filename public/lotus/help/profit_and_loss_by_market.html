P&L Report by Market

This section describes how to get more information about your performance and that of your MAs, Agents or Members for a market in a given time period.

Specify a time frame using the From and To drop-down menus and click the Search button to display the P&L Report by Market.

The report above shows that the Member lost (Win: -20) on this bet so their P&L is -20. This implies that the Agent, the MA and you won 4 each and that you passed up 8 to your upline.

To find out more about a market, click View Bets .

The table displays a breakdown of the profit and loss for each bet.
