P&L Report by Agent

This section describes how to get more information about the aggregated performance of a specific MA, Agent or Member on all available markets in a given time period.

Specify a time frame using the From and To drop-down menus and click the Search button to display the P&L Report by Agent.

The report above shows that the Member lost and paid their upline 20. The {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} paid 12 to you. You kept 4 and paid up 8 to the Company.

Clicking on the MA, it is shown that the Agent paid up 16 to them. The {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} kept 4 and paid up 12 to you.

When clicking on the Agent, it is revealed that there were actually two Members that lost 10 each. The Agent kept 4 (2 from each Member) and passed up 16 (8 for each Member) to their upline.

You can quickly access the list of bets the Members placed by clicking View Bets.

The table displays a breakdown of the profit and loss for each bet.
