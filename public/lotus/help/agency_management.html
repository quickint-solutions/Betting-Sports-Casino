{{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Management

This section describes how to perform basic user management tasks, such as creating downline users and setting or editing their account properties.

You can access this feature by navigating to the Agency Management > {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Listing menu.

The {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Listing window gives you a compact table of your MAs showing the most relevant user information, such as:
Login name and ID,
Downline button,
User status,
Downline summary button,
Net exposure,
Settled balance,
Credit limit and
Position Taking.

Settled balance is shown in two separate columns. The green number in the Take column shows how much the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} owes you, while the red in the Give column shows how much you owe the specific MA.

Click the arrow on the right side of the compact view to extend it and show further details about your MAs, including:
The date when the user account was created,
The date of the last successful login and
The last IP address used to log in with the user account.
