Reports

This section describes the reports that are available in the site .

Reports can help you find out more about your performance and that of your MAs, Agents or Members and about each market your downline users are engaged with.

You can view the reports by clicking the Reports menu in the menubar on the left.

Select the P&L Report by Market option to find out about your performance and that of your MAs, Agents or Members for a market in a given time period.
Select the P&L Report by Agent option to find out about the aggregated performance of a specific MA, Agent or Member on all available markets in a given time period.
Select the Bet List option to list all the bets the Members have placed in a given time period.
Select the Transfer Statement option to display all incoming or outgoing transfers that have taken place in the last 60 days.
