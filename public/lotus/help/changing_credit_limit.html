Changing the Credit Limit for an {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Account

This section describes how to change the credit limit for an {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}}.

When changing the credit limit of an MA, credit will be transferred to their balance or withdrawn from it.

Therefore, when increasing the credit limit for them, you must make sure that you have enough credit on your balance to transfer from.
Similarly, you should check the balance of the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} to make sure they have enough credit before decreasing their credit limit.

Also, you cannot withdraw more credit from an {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} than the sum you have distributed to them.

To change the credit limit, follow this procedure:

Navigate to the Agency Management > {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} Listing menu item and click the Login Name or the ID of the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}} to open their settings pane.

In the settings pane, go to the Credit section and set a new credit limit in the Credit Limit field.

Click the Save button to confirm the new values for the {{ctrl.$stateParams.user}}.
