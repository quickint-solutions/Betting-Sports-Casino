Admin Users

This section describes the role of users created by the Company a Customer and how to get basic information about them.

Customers are advised to create and maintain a list of users that are tasked to run the business and act on behalf of the Customers.

You can view your existing users by navigating to the Admin Users > User Listing menu item.
