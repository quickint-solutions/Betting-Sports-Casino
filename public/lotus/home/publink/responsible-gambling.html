Stay in Control, It’s Only A Game

We believe that gaming should always be an enjoyable activity and our customers can have fun from betting. Please kindly remember these following tips, it may help you to keep in control and make sure gaming will not become a problem.

  1. Only place bets with amounts that you can afford to lose.
  2. Gambling should be entertaining and not be seen as a way of making money.
  3. Bet sensibly and never chase your losses.
  4. Keep track of the time and amount you spend.
  5. Balance gambling with other activities. Think about if you are still having fun from betting if gaming is your only entertainment.
  6. Take regular breaks from gambling. You can use your self-exclusion option and our well-trained staff will call you to confirm your decision.
  7. Don’t gamble when under the abnormal conditions such as alcohol or when you’re upset.
Getting Help

  1. Players who are below the age of 18 are prohibited from gambling.
  2. We have measures to refuse under-age betting. Each person who wants to sign up for a new account at {{webname}} must tick the box ensuring they are over 18 years old. The registration will be rejected if they do not tick the box.
  3. Each person should fill in detailed personal information including name, date of birth, address to make sure that the person is over 18 years.
  4. The payment services of {{webname}} are based on mobile money. Players have to be aged 18 or above to register such services and they must have a valid national ID card.
  5. Other suggestions in order to avoid having minors from gambling at {{webname}}:
    1. Do not share details such as: username, password, among other sensitive information.
    2. Lock your computer with a password if you are using a public or shared computer.
    3. Enlighten your kids on any potential risks concerning gambling.

    2. CYBERsitter : a filtering software which allows parents to choose which websites to block.
    3. GamBlock : a specific blocking software for gambling websites. Also available for Android devices.
Problem Recognition

{{webname}} believe the majority of players enjoy the experience from betting in a responsible manner. However, for some reason, a small number of players might have a harmful impact from gambling. By answering such self-assessment questions below, it will help you consider if you have a problem:

  1. When you lose money, do you feel lost or depressed?
  2. When you lose money, do you want to gamble again to win money back ASAP?
  3. Do you run out of all money on betting in your account?
  4. Have you borrowed money from others and spend on betting?
  5. Have you lost interest in your family, friends or hobbies because of betting?
  6. Do you ever feel suicidal because of betting?
